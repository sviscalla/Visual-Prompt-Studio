Create a drawing using pastel as a dry medium to make the colors look calm and somewhat blended but not enough that you can’t tell the shapes. There should be movement of the wind in this piece shown by moving grass or hair of leaves or things that would normally blow in the wind. Give contrast from the subject and the rest of the world around him by making the subject dark while the world around him is light. The lines in the painting should be distinct as to show the shapes but otherwise unseen by our eyes giving the drawing a somewhat realistic feeling. Next, make the shapes be blended like the lines and the colors but not too much that they are impossible to tell where one shape starts and the other ends. Bring out the pastel coloring’s intense and velvety color as well as its soft edges. There should be a unity of where your eyes are first drawn. The drawing should help you to take it in all as one rather than piece by piece. The drawing should be made using soft pastel blocks and should use the technique of layering. The drawing subject should be a horse in a spring field with blue skies. Preferably a black horse that is normal but in the summer’s day and the outdoor light, is majestic.
