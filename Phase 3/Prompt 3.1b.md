Make a stone statue from the time period of Ancient Egypt that uses art as divine power.  The emphasis should be on the divine power in the artwork. The stone used should be diorite stone and this will give shades of gray and brown to the artwork. The texture should be smooth and it should be a 3 dimensional figure. There should be no open space as it is a statue and open space should be cut off. This statue is on a large scale and should be bigger than an average sized person. The statue should be located in or around the worshiping places of Ancient Egyptians to religiously inspire them.
