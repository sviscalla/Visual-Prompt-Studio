Make a medieval painting that conveys the themes of visionary and mystical experiences. This painting should be a tempera painting on canvas. The focus should be on the person having the vision and that is where your eyes should first be drawn and the focus can also be on the scribe writing the vision down. The colors should be shades of brown with red and light blue. These will make the subjects of the painting stand out from the brown background. The texture should be rough and there should be some depth but not too much. There should be little open space in this painting. It is filled with the subjects and details leaving almost no blank space. It should be small scale and be located in or around a church to remind people of the visionary theme it conveys. The artwork was created to inspire others and give hope and to be a religious symbol.
